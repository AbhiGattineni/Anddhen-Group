Privacy Policy for Word Highlighter

Last updated: May 28, 2025

Word Highlighter collects the following user data:
- Words and colors added by the user for highlighting purposes.
- This data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API.

We use this data to:
- Highlight user-specified words on webpages in the active tab.

We also fetch default word lists and highlight rules from our API at https://server.anddhengroup.com/api/default-words/. This API is hosted on our personal website and does not share your data with third parties. All API requests are made over HTTPS for security.

We do not share your data with any other third parties. You can contact us at anddhenconsulting@gmail.com for any privacy-related concerns.
